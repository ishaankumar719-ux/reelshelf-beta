# ReelShelf Product Bible
Version 1.0

This document is the foundation for every future design and product decision.

Do not treat this as a feature specification.

Treat it as the philosophy of ReelShelf.

------------------------------------------------------------

OUR MISSION

ReelShelf exists to help people discover, remember and share the stories that shape them.

Not just movies.

Not just television.

Not just books.

Stories.

Everything we build should reinforce this mission.

------------------------------------------------------------

OUR PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY

We are NOT building another media tracker.

We are building the most beautiful home for people who love stories.

ReelShelf should feel curated.

Intentional.

Personal.

Editorial.

Calm.

Premium.

Emotional.

The interface should disappear.

The stories should become the hero.

------------------------------------------------------------

OUR NORTH STAR

Every feature must answer one question:

"Would this make someone open ReelShelf even if they weren't planning to log anything today?"

If the answer is no,

rethink it.

------------------------------------------------------------

OUR FOUR PILLARS

DISCOVER

Help people discover stories they never knew they wanted.

Examples:

Daily Reel

Collections

Mood Discovery

Hidden Gems

Editorial Picks

Cross-media journeys

------------------------------------------------------------

REMEMBER

Help people build a lifetime shelf.

Examples:

Diary

Ratings

Reviews

Year in Review

Reading history

Watch history

Personal stats

------------------------------------------------------------

SHARE

Help people connect through taste.

Examples:

Shelf Match

Community

Collections

Friends

Shared recommendations

Discussion

------------------------------------------------------------

CURATE

Algorithms assist.

Humans curate.

ReelShelf should never feel like an endless recommendation engine.

Editorial curation always wins.

------------------------------------------------------------

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Imagine if these brands collaborated:

Apple

A24

MUBI

Kinfolk

Monocle

Wallpaper*

The interface should feel expensive without being flashy.

Premium through restraint.

------------------------------------------------------------

VISUAL LANGUAGE

Large editorial typography.

Clean sans-serif interface.

Dark cinematic surfaces.

Warm lighting.

Beautiful poster imagery.

Soft gradients.

Layered depth.

Minimal borders.

Generous spacing.

Every screen breathes.

------------------------------------------------------------

MOTION

Motion should feel calm.

Nothing should bounce aggressively.

Everything should ease.

Cards gently lift.

Headers fade.

Sections glide.

Scrolling should feel like turning pages in a magazine.

------------------------------------------------------------

COLOUR

Black.

Warm charcoal.

Soft white.

Muted grey.

Green is only an accent.

Gold only appears for editorial moments.

Artwork provides the colour.

------------------------------------------------------------

TYPOGRAPHY

Typography creates emotion.

Serif only where emotion matters.

Sans-serif everywhere else.

Large hierarchy.

Confident whitespace.

------------------------------------------------------------

VOICE

Never shout.

Never use clickbait.

Never feel algorithmic.

Write like an editor.

Not a marketer.

Examples:

"Stories worth your time."

"Hand-picked by ReelShelf."

"One unforgettable recommendation."

Not:

"Trending Now!!!"

------------------------------------------------------------

COMMUNITY

We are not building social media.

We are building a community around stories.

Meaningful discussion over engagement.

Taste over popularity.

Collections over feeds.

Shared discovery over endless scrolling.

------------------------------------------------------------

THE REELSHELF EXPERIENCE

Opening ReelShelf should feel like walking into the world's most beautifully curated independent cinema and bookshop.

Not browsing a database.

------------------------------------------------------------

PRODUCT RULES

Every screen should contain one moment of delight.

Every interaction should feel premium.

Every recommendation should feel intentional.

Every collection should tell a story.

Every profile should reveal personality.

Every animation should have purpose.

Nothing exists simply because other apps have it.

------------------------------------------------------------

SUCCESS

People should eventually describe ReelShelf as:

"The most beautiful place to discover stories."

Not:

"The best app for tracking movies."

------------------------------------------------------------

FROM TODAY

Every design decision.

Every new feature.

Every Claude prompt.

Every screen.

Must follow this Product Bible.
